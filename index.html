Moxfield Tag Sorter

How to use:

  1. Go to your Moxfield deck page
  2. Click on "Bulk Edit" in the deck editor
  3. Copy the entire deck list (includes set codes and tags)
  4. Paste the deck list in the text area below
  5. View your sorted deck by tags in table or CSV format
